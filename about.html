About Youth Medical Association

Passion. Perceive. Pursue.

A global nonprofit empowering the next generation of healthcare leaders through education, mentorship, and hands-on experience.

Our Story

From Vision to Global Impact

Youth Medical Association was founded by Rayhan Mehal, a student at The Bronx High School of Science, driven by a firsthand understanding of healthcare's transformative power.

At age four, a hospital stay revealed to him the dedication and compassion of medical professionals. This experience, combined with his grandmother's work as a physician, inspired a lifelong commitment to medicine and to creating opportunities for young people everywhere.

Upon entering a specialized high school, he observed stark disparities in funding and opportunities across NYC schools. Students from other districts shared how difficult it was to access career opportunities and internships. The vision became clear: every student deserves equitable access to pursue their interests.

YMA was founded to bridge this gap, creating a supportive global community where aspiring healthcare professionals can thrive regardless of their background.

Our Programs

What We Do

Department of Career Development

Beyond medicine: our Career Development department supports students in computer science, engineering, law, and business. We partner with Columbia Engineering and host events across diverse fields.

Educational Programs

Weekly meetings featuring discussions on medical specialties, current public health issues, and presentations by medical professionals.

Mentorship Network

We connect high school students with college students, medical students, and practicing physicians for guidance and career advice.

Hands-On Experiences

Hospital visits, shadowing experiences, and practical training (including CPR certification) led by experienced professionals.

Leadership Development

Leadership roles and team positions that develop organizational and management skills essential for future professionals.

Community Outreach

Community service and fundraising initiatives that give back while building character and civic engagement.

College Preparation

Guidance on college applications, pre-med programs, and medical school preparation through our experienced mentors.

By the Numbers

Our Growing Impact

Since our founding, Youth Medical Association has grown into a global community of over 4,000 students across 40+ chapters worldwide.

Our partnerships with Columbia Engineering, NYU Langone, the New York Fire Department (FDNY), and the Sophie Davis Biomedical Education Program provide members with unparalleled opportunities across medicine, research, and beyond.

We maintain a supportive, collaborative environment where students explore their path, develop leadership skills, and build lasting connections with peers and mentors across the globe.
