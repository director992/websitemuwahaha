With 32 active chapters spanning across 4 continents, YMA International connects passionate students worldwide in their pursuit of medical excellence.

Global Presence

Our international chapters serve as local hubs for medical education, bringing together students from diverse backgrounds to share knowledge, experiences, and aspirations in healthcare.

Each chapter is led by dedicated student leaders who organize events, coordinate with local medical institutions, and foster a supportive community for aspiring healthcare professionals.
