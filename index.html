Our Mission

We support young people in pursuing their careers across medicine, research, engineering, law, business, computer science, and related fields.

Through partnerships with institutions like Columbia Engineering, NYU Langone, and others, we help students develop skills and gain hands-on experience across many fields.

What We Offer

Programs for students exploring careers in medicine, research, engineering, law, business, and related fields.

Medical Mentorship

Connect with practicing physicians, medical students, and healthcare professionals who provide guidance on career paths, academic preparation, and professional development.

Clinical Workshops

Hands-on workshops covering essential medical skills, from basic life support to advanced diagnostic techniques, led by experienced healthcare professionals.

Hospital Visits

Behind-the-scenes tours of medical facilities, with opportunities to observe procedures and interact with medical teams.

CPR & First Aid Training

Certified training programs in CPR, AED use, and first aid, providing essential life-saving skills and official certifications.

Leadership Development

Leadership training that helps develop communication, teamwork, and organizational skills for healthcare.

Research Opportunities

Access to research projects and scientific studies, providing experience in medical research methodology and scientific publication.

Department of Career Development

Not just medicine. Our Career Development team supports students interested in computer science, engineering, law, and business too. We've partnered with Columbia Engineering and host events across many fields.

Recent Activities

Events and partnerships across medicine, research, engineering, and more. We've worked with Columbia Engineering, NYU, and others.

Hospital & Medical Facility Visits

Members visit medical institutions to gain hands-on experience and learn from healthcare professionals in real clinical settings.

Hands-On Medical Training

Students participate in practical medical training sessions, learning essential skills and techniques under the guidance of experienced professionals.

Community Building & Networking

We foster a supportive community where students pursuing medicine, research, engineering, and other fields connect and build lasting relationships.

Columbia Engineering & Career Development

Our Department of Career Development expands beyond medicine. We've partnered with Columbia Engineering and host events for students interested in engineering, computer science, law, and business.

Bake Sales & Community Fundraising

YMA chapters host bake sales and fundraising events to support medical education programs and community outreach initiatives.
